# App ID

**mizu App ID** is a unique identifier for your app. It is used to identify your app when you interact with the `@mizuwallet-sdk/core`.

<Callout>
  NOTICE: **NOT** all dApps require an App ID when integrating with MizuWallet.
  Especially for small or startup projects. See
  [here](/docs/using-aptos-wallet-adapter/extension-dapps) to integrate with
  MizuWallet via
  [@aptos-labs/aptos-wallet-adapter](https://github.com/aptos-labs/aptos-wallet-adapter).
</Callout>

## How to get your mizu App ID?

1. Go to Telegram.
2. Create a new bot. [Read More](/docs/using-aptos-wallet-adapter/telegram-miniApps/telegram-mini-app-development)
3. Save your **bot token**.
4. Fill up the [Form](https://ankaalabs.typeform.com/to/bAPqSxDu).
