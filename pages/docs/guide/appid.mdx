# App ID

**App ID** is a unique identifier for your app. It is used to identify your app when you interact with the `@mizuwallet-sdk/core`.

## How to get your App ID?

1. Go to Telegram.
2. Create a new bot. [Read More](/docs/guide/telegram)
3. Save your **bot token**.
4. Exchange your **bot token** with your **App ID** by contacting us.
